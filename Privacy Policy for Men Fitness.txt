•	Ad Providers may use cookies or other tracking technologies to gather information about your interaction with the advertisements, but this information is not shared with us, and we do not have access to it.
5. Security
Since we do not collect personal information, there are no security concerns related to user data. However, we take standard industry measures to ensure the app operates securely and functions properly.
